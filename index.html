Coastal Bliss

Varkala

Famous for its dramatic red cliffs overlooking the Arabian Sea, Varkala offers a unique blend of spirituality and sun-drenched relaxation. The Papanasam beach is known for its holy waters.

Backwaters

Alleppey (Alappuzha)

The 'Venice of the East' is best experienced on a traditional houseboat. Navigate through emerald green lagoons, coconut groves, and witness the charming rural life of Kerala.

Royal Heritage

Hari Mahal Palace, Jaipur

Experience the grandeur of Rajputana at the Hari Mahal Palace. With its stunning architecture, lush gardens, and royal hospitality, it offers a glimpse into the opulent lifestyle of Jaipur's royalty.

Mountain Retreat

Manali

Nestled in the Beas River Valley, Manali is a gateway for adventure and tranquility. From snow-capped peaks at Solang Valley to the charming Old Manali streets, it's a paradise for every traveler.

Wildlife

Thekkady

Home to the Periyar National Park, Thekkady is a haven for nature lovers. Explore spice plantations, enjoy bamboo rafting, and catch a glimpse of wild elephants by the lake.
